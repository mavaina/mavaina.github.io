About me

My name is Laima Augutytė. I am a third year psychology student. Hovever I am passionate about performing arts. I love theater, new circus (especialy clowning) and dance. Currently I take part in Klaipeda's pantomime theater "A". As well I am involved in project called "Jungtys" which focus on integration of people with disabilities though dance. I have studied in Danish højskole "Performers House" for a year in 2012-2013. As well I have attended a few performing projects and attended some seminars. In general apreciate arts and I love performing and collaborating with people. Finally, I believe in a power of art. So, in a future I would like to incorporate both my psychological knowledge and my expierience with performing into my future career.
